GHOSTS

How to play

Two players are required to start the game.

When two clients are connected, click the ready button to prompt the game to start.

Move around with the arrow keys and use the space bar to attack.

Enemies

Ghosts will spawn frequently, so be sure to move around!

When a ghost spawns, they will randomly choose one of the players to follow.

Attacks

A ghost needs to be hit three times in order to be killed. The range of your attack covers every square around you.

Whenever a ghost does a counter attack, a coloured circle will be seen around the ghost. Grey indicates a miss hit and orange indicates a hit, which will make the player take damage.

Health

If both players' health reaches zero the game is over.

Medicine packs will spawn randomly across the map which can be picked up simply by walking over them. They will regenerate some of the player's health.

Enjoy your game!
